Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Free Insured Shipping

We offer free insured shipping on all orders within India. Your precious jewellery is fully insured during transit.

Delivery Time

Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. Customized orders may take 10-15 business days.

International Shipping

We ship internationally. Shipping charges and customs duties are calculated at checkout and are the responsibility of the customer.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS to track your package.

Signature Required

All deliveries require a signature upon receipt to ensure safe delivery of your valuable jewellery.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to PO Box addresses. A physical address is required for delivery.
